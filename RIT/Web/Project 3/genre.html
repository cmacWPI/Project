Anime Genres

List of Anime Genres
Genre What is this anime genre?
Action Most common genre; however anime with this genre tends to lack character development or decent storyline. It is often mixed with seveal other genres to make it interesting to turn into good storyline.
Example of this genre is Naruto.
Adventure This type of genre deals with characters moving and seaching for new places. Animes with this genre tend to long, yet a good storyline; however unlike other genre, the story progress slowly and can bores viewers out. This genre tends to mix with other genres such as action, comedy, etc.
Example of this genre is Pokemon.
Comedy This type of genre tend to have good character development, along with good character design and often comes with humor.
Example of this genre is Lucky Star.
Drama This type of genre tend to have sometimes emotional, suspense, etc. It can give its viewers a feeling of it.
Example of this genre is Kanon (2006).
Ecchi This type of genre is used as "fanservice" and often shown some hint of sexual however it doesn't show at all if not, at most..
Example of this genre is To Love-Ru.
Fantasy This type of genre tend to have a magical world, powers, and sometimes often involves monsters in it. It is sometimes backed up by folklore or mythlogy; however most of them aren't present although does mix with other genre to back it up.
Example of this genre is Hunter x Hunter
Horror This type of genre tend to have anime sometimes show bloodshed, gruesome scenes, etc.
Example of this genre is Fate/Zero.
Magic This type of genre often involves girl with wands, if not, tends to involve people who use magic powers, etc.
Example of this genre is Mahou Shoujo Lyrical Nanoha.
Mystery This type of genre tends to have series of event chains to another and is often left unexplained. Sometimes it involves the main character tries to solve the case and attempts to find an answer to case step by step.
Example of this genre is Death Note.
Psychological One of best anime genre, tends to leaves viewers at edge of whats happening next. It tends to have a lot of twist along with superb storyline.
Example of this genre is Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion.
Romance This type of genre is one of popular genre that deals with character's relationship with another. It deals with progression of love between two characters.
Example of this genre is Clannad.
Sci-Fi Sci-Fi aka Science Fiction, this type of genre involves with technology, sometimes takes place in future, or at space. Sci-Fi is mostly used with Mecha theme.
Example of this genre is Gundam Seed.
Slice Of Life This type of genre involves character's daily part of life, if not whole.
Example of this genre is School Rumble
Supernatural This type of genre involves a lot of demons, spirits, and other beings not in this world such as aliens, etc
Example of this genre is Suzumiya Haruhi no Yuuutsu (The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya)
