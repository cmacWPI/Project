Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods Character #4 - Whis

This character page is about Whis in Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods.

General Infomation

Whis is Birus's martial arts's teacher and is the person who trained him to be successful as God of Destruction of the Seventh Universe. He is stronger than Birus, althouhg he has calm and peaceful personality.

Movie Biography

Whis was first seen when he was visiting Birus in his temple and tells him to wake up. He stated he will sing with microphone if he doesn't get up, Birus ended up waking up due to his terrible singing. He later was asked by Birus about Freiza destoryed Planet Vegeta, and he replied with yes, and told him with few more infomation stating that he died by at Goku in his Super Saiyan form. He also told him about their current whereabouts including Goku's location.

After they arrived in King Kai's planet, he reminded Birus to stop complaining as he was the reason who destoryed King Kai's planet after he lost in game. He simply doesn't particpate in any of fights, he just only observes and do nothing. After Birus defeated Goku, they both set destination to Earth and arrives. When Birus starts to fight Z Fighters, Whis is in restaurant eating sushi. He started to take a liking to sushi and bought to carry back to home. After Birus's fight was over, he offered Goku the position as God of Destruction, but was turned down. They returned back to home, and he later shared his sushi with Birus. After Birus tried it, which caused him to destory several moons in temple, he karate-chops him in his neck and told him to go to sleep for three more years.
