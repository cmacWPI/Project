Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods Character #2 - Vegeta

This character page is about Vegeta in Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods.

General Infomation

His name is Vegeta, sometime known as Prince Vegeta, as prince of fallen Saiyan race.

Movie Biography

Few years later after Majin Buu, Vegeta was training until King Kai warned him about Birus's arrival to Earth(He did not attend his wife's birthday party at that time, since he wanted to train). Upon hearing this, he went to Bulma's birthday party to warn everyone, but Birus arrived before he could do that. After being face-to-face once agian, he knew Birus is very dangerous and is ready to cast aside his pride to put him in good mood and starts playing party-host.

It failed when Majinn Buu angered Birus, and Vegeta tried to fight him, but is defeated. He admitted defeat and when he was about to be finished off, Bulma arrived in the scene and scolds Birus telling him that he ruined her birthday party and then slapped him. Birus later returns favor and slapped her back, causing Vegeta to explode in rage, which allows him to be stronger than Goku for a moment. He went to fight Birus again and after his rage calmed down, Birus is left unscathed. He was about to accept his fate along with planet's fate, but Goku steps in and he helped Goku to reach Super Super Saiyan God form. After the fight, he demands Goku to help him to reach Super Saiyan God this time and was being needled by Goku when he shouted his wife's name after Birus slapped her.
