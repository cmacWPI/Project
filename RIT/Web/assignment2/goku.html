Movie Biography

Few years later after Majin Buu, he trains on King Kai's planet. During his training, he overhears an discussion from King Kai, and later learns about Birus being powerful foe far stronger than Majin Buu. Eager about it, he wishes to challenge him. Upon challenging Birus on King Kai's planet, he is overwhelmed and lost in two hits in his Super Saiyan 3 form.

Upon his defeat, he went to Earth and watches as Vegeta was fighting Birus, but does it to form strategy, however it was useless. He later used Dragon Balls to summon Shenlong and asked him how to create Super Saiyan God. With the help of other five pure-hearted Saiyans, he exceeds the form beyond Super Sayian 3 and became Super Saiyan God.

Upon reaching the form, he confronts Birus again and they were evenly matched. However, he returned back to normal after time limt ran out, but he did not notice as he has already absorbed Super Saiyan God's power into his body. The battle went on until Birus wins upon using his technique, God of Destruction Super Energy Sphere. He barely can hold back his techinque, but later turns to Super Saiyan God and then absorbed his energy sphere.

Having acknowledged Birus's power is far beyond his level, he admitted defeat which ended the fight. Whis offered him a job to be next God of Destruction if Birus dies, but he declined Whis's offer, and Supereme Kais was surprised that Goku manage to befriended Birus.
