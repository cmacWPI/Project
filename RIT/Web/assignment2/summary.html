Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods Brief Summary

In here, you will find small brief summary about the movie, the summary may be long, it tells where and when movie took place and what happens in it afterwards. Read on to find out! Do note, beware of spoliers.

Summary/Plot

The story took place five years later after Majin Buu's defeat, which determines the fate of whole universe. There was a far stronger enemey out there, far stronger than the villians that Z Fighters have ever faced. Birus (due to fan-translations, some call him Bills, other called him Beerus), the God of Destruction has woken from his long 39-years slumber.

Birus, after woken from his long slumber, was told by Whis and much to his surprise that Freiza was killed at hands of Son Goku. Hearing the news about it with mentioned "Super Saiyan", he became interested and recalled his prophetic dream where he encounted an being known as "Super Saiyan God". 39 years ago, before his slumber, Oracle Fish told him that an mighty opponent would appeared before him as "Super Saiyan God".

Whis told him that majority of Sayians live on "Planet 4032-877" which is Earth, however told him Goku is on King Kai's planet. Birus set off to fight him. Prior to Birus's awakening, Goku was excited to challenge him after learning things about God of Destruction was a powerful foe from King Kai. When Birus arrived on King Kai's planet, Goku challenged him however, he was defeated in two strikes at his Super Saiyan 3 form. Birus was disappointed, due to lack of challenge and sets off to Earth, hoping for powerful foe in Earth. King Kai warned Vegeta right away about Birus's upcoming arrival to Earth. Upon hearing this, he was prepared to cast his pride aside to put him in good mood. He arrived and interupted Bulma's birthday party.

Upon arriving, he casually greeted Vegeta and his wife, and particpated in her birthday party and is enjoying it. After two incidents, where the gun bullet hits Birus's forehead and Buu ate all of pudding in front of his face, he proceeds to destory Earth. The Z fighters quicky notices it and then tries to fight him, but later was all defeated easily. Vegeta's rage became stronger than Goku when Birus slapped Bulma, was able to fight him, but is stil defeatd by him.

Goku arrived the scene and summoned Shenlong to tell him how to make Super Saiyan God, and Shenlong proceeds to tell him about the legend of Super Saiyan God in Planet Vegeta and how he appeared to stop evil Saiyans but failed. He later proceeds to tell him that to turn into Super Saiyan God, it requires powers of six pure-hearted Saiyans, and thanks to help from them, he was able to turn into Super Saiyan God which is far stronger than Super Saiyan 3 and also fufilled Oracle Fish's prophecy.

Having turned into Super Saiyan God, he was able to fight Birus in equal footing, however Birus pushed him back with his strongest attack. During the crisis, he had a flashback and turned back into god form, which he absorbed energy ball. He, however, told to him that he lost and the fight was over, and Birus told him two interesting infomation. Having said that, and bid farewell, Birus returned to his temple, but Whis knocks him out and told him to go to sleep for three years.
